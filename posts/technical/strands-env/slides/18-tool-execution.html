Tool Execution

Tool calls are a big share of the wallclock — the bottleneck differs by environment:

SearchEnv
Many APIs like web search are highly rate-limited — fire too many requests at once and the provider throttles you.
→ a semaphore and/or a rate_limiter caps concurrency under the limit
CodeEnv
Every task initializes, runs, and cleans up a new sandbox — and a long-running code can take minutes.
→ cap active sessions, enforce backpressure and timeouts, and preserve faithful execution results across retries and cleanup
