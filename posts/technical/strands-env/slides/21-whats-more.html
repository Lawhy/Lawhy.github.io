What's More

Say one observes a broken reward curve, what could go wrong?

Data tasks too easy, too hard, or low quality — no usable gradient.
Rolloutthis talk the environment never produced a faithful rollout — token drift, uninformative tool feedback, stale policy, etc.
Verifier inaccurate, hackable, or flipping on equivalent answers — the target itself is wrong.
Algorithm only after the above — KL, advantage estimation, learning rate.

↵It's tempting to blame the algorithm. Usually it's upstream.
