Chat Template Rendering

But chat templates (on Hugging Face) don't always cooperate...

What breaks
✗ Re-template the entire trajectory each turn → rewrites model-generated tokens (breaks TITO)
✗ Template the new message alone → model-specific artifacts (e.g. GLM injects [gMASK]<sop>)
What we do
1
Fake-prefix trick — covers many templates
Diff a templated fake context against fake + msg to extract just the new chunk:
new_ids = encode(msg_sepeverything between <|im_end|> and next <|im_start|> — e.g. "\n" for Qwen + template(fake + msg) − template(fake))
2
Manual template patches — for the rest
Monkey-patch the model's chat_template per family: skip BOS, remove single-message restrictions, etc.
