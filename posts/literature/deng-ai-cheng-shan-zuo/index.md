<div class="poem">
<p class="poem__line">霂雨酥城间<sup class="margin-marker"><a href="#g-mu">1</a></sup><span class="margin-note" id="g-mu"><span class="margin-note__label">霂 · mù</span>细雨；蒙蒙细雨。</span></p>
<p class="poem__line">沥凉风微倾</p>
<p class="poem__line">向山出阁世</p>
<p class="poem__line">寻海问仙程</p>
<p class="poem__line">浑天承墙邑<sup class="margin-marker"><a href="#g-yi">2</a></sup><span class="margin-note" id="g-yi"><span class="margin-note__label">邑 · yì</span>城邑；都市。此处指爱丁堡。</span></p>
<p class="poem__line">黯色庇静幽</p>
<p class="poem__line">云低可曾触</p>
<p class="poem__line">怀情翥上空<sup class="margin-marker"><a href="#g-zhu">4</a></sup><span class="margin-note" id="g-zhu"><span class="margin-note__label">翥 · zhù</span>鸟向上飞，引申为情思高举。</span></p>
