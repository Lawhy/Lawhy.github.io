The **Graph Neural Network** (GNN) implements a function $$\tau(\mathcal{G}, n) \in \mathbb{R}^m$$ that maps a graph $$\mathcal{G}$$ and one of its node $$n$$ into an $$m$$-dimensional Euclidean space.
