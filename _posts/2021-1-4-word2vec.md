**Skip-gram**

- *Objective Function*: Let the target word embedding be $$w_t$$ and the context word embedding be $$w_c$$, then the closeness $$u_c$$ between $$w_t$$ and $$w_c$$ can be computed by *dot-product* such that $$u_c = w_c \cdot w_t$$.
