**Embedding** is given by some mathematical function \\(f: X \rightarrow Y\\) which is `injective`, `structure-preserving` and often 
maps from the abstract high dimensional space to the concrete low diemnsional space to resolve the *sparsity* and *fractured information* of the one-hot encoding.
Here \\(X\\) is said to be embedded in \\(Y\\).
